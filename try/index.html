Artificial intelligence, also known as AI, is the creation of computer systems that are capable of carrying out tasks that would typically require human intelligence, such as comprehending natural language, identifying sounds and images, and making judgments based on data. Narrow AI and general AI are the two main subfields of AI. Narrow AI is created to carry out particular tasks, like speech or image recognition. On the other hand, general AI seeks to develop a system that is capable of carrying out any intellectual task that a human can.
